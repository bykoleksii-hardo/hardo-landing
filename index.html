FAQ

The honest answers.

01Is this just ChatGPT in a wrapper?
No. The question pool is hand-curated by analysts and associates from BB and EB shops. The grading rubric was calibrated against ~200 real first-round and superday transcripts. The AI runs the session; humans set the bar.
02How realistic is the "pressure" really?
A 60-90s answer window, an unprompted follow-up the moment you finish, and an MD-style "okay — but what if rates rise 200bps?" cut-in. Most users describe their first session as harder than their actual first round.
03Which banks and groups are covered?
14 bulge brackets, 9 elite boutiques, and 17 middle-market shops, with coverage- and product-group presets (M&A, Restructuring, LevFin, Industrials, TMT, Healthcare, FIG, Real Estate). New presets added weekly during recruiting season.
04Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Monthly billing, no minimum term. Cancel from the dashboard; access continues until the end of the current period. We don't run retention scripts.
05What about behavioral / fit interviews?
Covered. The behavioral pool grades on STAR structure, specificity, and the "why this firm / why this group" answer. Same scorecard format as technicals, different rubric.
06Do you store my recordings?
Audio is processed for transcription and grading and deleted within 24 hours. Transcripts and scorecards stay in your account so you can track progress. Delete the account, delete the data — same day.
